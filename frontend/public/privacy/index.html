Privacy Policy

Data Processing

This application processes your financial data locally on your machine.

No data is transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Data Storage

All uploaded files are temporary and stored only for the duration of processing.

Files are automatically deleted after the report is generated.

We do not store any of your financial data on our servers.

Data Security

Your data security is our priority. The application:

  • Processes data locally on your machine
  • Does not transmit data over the internet
  • Uses temporary files that are automatically cleaned up
  • Does not collect or store any personal information

Third-Party Services

This application does not use any third-party services for data processing.

All processing happens locally using open-source libraries.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store any of your data, the following rights apply:

  • You have full control over your data
  • You can delete your data at any time by clearing temporary files
  • You can review the source code to understand how data is processed

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
